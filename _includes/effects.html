Expected Effects

Positive Changes HelloWorld Will Create

Improved Information Accessibility for Foreigners

Through legal consultations on unfair treatment, we can protect the rights of foreign workers and reduce unfair practices. By providing information on visa extensions, we can help with legal stays and prevent residence-related issues. Easy access to necessary information such as job opportunities and relevant regulations facilitates job-seeking activities.

Promoting Social Integration

By providing legal employment opportunities and residence information, we can reduce illegal stay issues. Through protecting the rights of foreign workers and providing information, we can help their adaptation to Korean society and promote social integration.
